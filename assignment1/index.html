About Us

We started House Plant Shop as a group of childhood friends who all shared a love for nature and the outdoors. After college, we became roommates and quickly realized that filling our home with plants was something we all had in common.

What started in our shared home has now grown into something much bigger. Today, we operate out of a large greenhouse and continue bringing healthy plants to plant lovers everywhere.
