Succulent Glossary

Farina
The powdery, waxy coating on succulent leaves that acts as a natural sunscreen and repels water.
Rosette
A circular, petal-like leaf arrangement common in Echeverias, designed to catch water and direct it to the roots.
Propagation
The highly rewarding process of creating entirely new succulents from fallen leaves or stem cuttings.

Care: Do's & Don'ts

✓ Soak & Dry

Water deeply until it drains, then wait for the soil to dry 100% before watering again.

✗ Don't Mist

Unlike ferns, succulents hate wet leaves. Misting causes rot and creates sunburn spots.

✓ Sun Stress

Provide plenty of bright light to bring out vibrant reds, pinks, and purples in the leaves.
