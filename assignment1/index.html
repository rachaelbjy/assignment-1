About Us

Cacti-Succulent Kuching started as a small project by a group of friends who share a strong interest in plants and nature. At first, we only kept plants for our own homes, but we soon realised that many people also enjoy having plants in their living spaces. This inspired us to start sharing our plants with others.

Over time, we turned our passion into a small business. Today, we offer a variety of cactus and succulent plants that are carefully selected and well-maintained. Our goal is to provide healthy plants and create a simple and enjoyable experience for our customers.
